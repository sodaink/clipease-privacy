Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

Thank you for choosing ClipEase. As a clipboard management tool, we understand that your data privacy is paramount. This policy covers data processing practices for ClipEase on macOS and iOS platforms.

Core Promise: Although ClipEase supports multi-device synchronization, your clipboard data is always stored on devices you control and within your private iCloud space. The developer cannot access any of your clipboard content.

1. Information Collection and Use

  • Clipboard Data: The app reads your clipboard to provide history, search, and quick paste features. This data is stored only in your local encrypted database.
  • iCloud Sync: This app uses Apple iCloud for data synchronization. Your data is transmitted directly to your private iCloud account via encrypted channels. The developer does not provide third-party servers and cannot decrypt or view this synced data.
  • No Personal Information: We do not have an account system and do not collect your name, ID, contact information, or unique device identifiers.
  • No Third-Party Analytics: This app does not integrate any third-party behavioral analysis or tracking SDKs. Your usage habits remain completely private.

2. Features and Data Processing

  • Search: The search index is built entirely locally and does not upload search terms to any server.
  • Auto-Cleanup: You can set a retention period for history. The app will automatically delete expired entries locally based on your settings to ensure efficient use of storage space.

3. Permissions

  • Clipboard Access: Used to implement core history management features.
  • Network Connection: Used exclusively for data synchronization with Apple iCloud servers.
  • Accessibility (macOS): If "Auto-Paste" is enabled, this permission is required to simulate commands. This operation is performed entirely locally.

4. Your Rights

You can clear all history data in the app settings at any time or turn off iCloud sync in system settings. Once deleted, data cannot be recovered.
